Professional Roles

Gerry has held many roles within various organizations and educational institutions, including faculty appointments at the University of British Columbia, Elder advisor appointments at post-secondary institutions, and consulting positions in health care, corrections, and others.

  • Elder Advisor, Sweat Lodge Keeper, and Guest Lecturer, British Columbia Institute of Technology - 1996 to 2013
  • Cultural Advisor, British Columbia Institute of Technology - 1996 to 1998

Elected Offices

Chief

Seton Lake Indian Band, three terms - 1978 to 1982, 1986 to 1988 Shalalth, British Columbia
  • Provided political, cultural and social leadership for community responsible for community administration, providing leadership to Band council, conducting meetings and consultations.
  • Represented community in regional, provincial and national forums member of Union of BC Indian Chiefs, ex offico with Assembly of First Nations Summit.
  • Established community's first elementary school and gymnasium through a consultative and participatory process with Elders, Council members and staff.
  • Developed an effective administration of programs and services.

Band Councillor

Seton Lake Indian Band - 1974 to 1978 Shalalth, British Columbia
  • Worked with Band Councillors and Chief to promote effective administration and leadership to community.
  • Chaired Council meetings, Education Committee, Recreation Committee, Social Development Committee.
  • Developed addictions programs.
